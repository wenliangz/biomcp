# BRAF (B-Raf proto-oncogene, serine/threonine kinase)

Entrez ID: 673
Chromosome: 7:140719327-140924976 (strand: -1)
UniProt: P15056
Type: protein-coding

## Summary

This gene encodes a protein belonging to the RAF family of serine/threonine protein kinases. This protein plays a role in regulating the MAP kinase/ERK signaling pathway, which affects cell division, differentiation, and secretion.
Diseases: cardiofaciocutaneous syndrome, Noonan syndrome, melanoma, Noonan syndrome with multiple lentigines, cancer
Drugs: ENCORAFENIB, VEMURAFENIB, REGORAFENIB, DABRAFENIB MESYLATE
